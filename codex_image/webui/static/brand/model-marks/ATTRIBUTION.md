# Model-family marks

These local SVGs are used only to identify the OpenAI and Google Gemini model families in iLab CONJURE. They are rendered as small, contextual model indicators and do not imply endorsement or a partnership.

The vector references were retrieved from the public `selfh.st/icons` and `SVG Logos` collections through [Iconify](https://iconify.design/), which respectively publish their collection data under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) and [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/). The underlying names and marks remain the property of their respective owners.

For current vendor usage requirements, consult [OpenAI Design](https://openai.com/brand/) and [Google Brand Resource Center](https://about.google/brand-resource-center/guidance/apis/).
